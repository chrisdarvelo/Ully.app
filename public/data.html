Data Usage

Last updated: February 21, 2026  ·  A plain-language breakdown of every data point Ully touches.

The Short Version

We collect one thing: your email address, to create your account. Everything else — your recipes, profile, saved cafes, chat history — lives on your device and never leaves it. We have no access to your conversations or content.

How AI Messages Are Handled

When you send a message or photo to Ully AI, here is exactly what happens:

  • Your message and any attached photo are sent from your device to a Firebase Cloud Function running on Google's servers
  • The Cloud Function forwards the message to Anthropic's Claude API for processing — your email or account ID is never included
  • Anthropic returns an AI-generated response
  • The response is delivered to your device
  • Neither we nor Firebase store the message content — it exists only in transit
  • Your conversation is saved only to your device's local storage

Per Anthropic's API data usage policy, your inputs are not used to train AI models. Anthropic retains data only briefly for trust and safety monitoring. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details.

Location Data

Ully requests your approximate location (not precise GPS) to fetch local weather conditions and provide weather-aware drink recommendations. Specifically:

  • We request "coarse" location — city-level, not street-level precision
  • Your location is passed to wttr.in, a public weather service, in each AI request that uses weather context
  • Your location is never stored by us or transmitted for any other purpose
  • You can deny location permission and Ully will function normally without weather context

Data We Do Not Sell or Share

We do not sell, rent, trade, license, or otherwise share your personal data with any third party for commercial, marketing, or advertising purposes.

We do not use: analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporting services, A/B testing tools, heatmap tools, session recording tools, or any other behavioral tracking systems.

Google Play Data Safety

In Google Play's Data Safety section, Ully discloses the following:

  • Data collected: Email address (account management, required)
  • Data shared: Messages and photos shared with Anthropic for AI processing (ephemeral, not stored)
  • Data encrypted in transit: Yes
  • Data deletion: Users can delete all data from within the app
  • No data sale: We do not sell any user data

Your Controls

  • Camera permission: Deny at any time in your device settings — other features remain unaffected
  • Location permission: Deny at any time — AI still works without weather context
  • Chat history: Clear at any time from within the app
  • Account & all data: Delete permanently from within the app at any time
