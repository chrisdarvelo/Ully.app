Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.

Why isn't the AI answering my question?

Ully AI is designed exclusively for coffee-related topics. Questions outside of coffee, brewing, equipment, or barista technique will not receive a response. Try rephrasing your question around a coffee context.

I hit the daily AI limit. What now?

Ully enforces a daily message limit to keep the service running smoothly for everyone. Your limit resets at midnight. If you believe you've hit the limit in error, contact us at support@ullycoffee.com.

I lost my recipes. Can they be recovered?

Recipes are stored locally on your device only. If you deleted the app, factory reset your phone, or deleted your account, they cannot be recovered from our end — they were never on our servers. We recommend exporting important recipes externally.

The Dial-in camera isn't working.

Make sure you've granted Ully camera permissions in your device settings. Go to Settings → Ully → Camera and enable access. Restart the app after granting permission.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the Profile tab, scroll to the bottom, and tap Delete Account. See our full Account Deletion guide for step-by-step instructions.

Does Ully store my conversations?

No. Your chat history is stored only on your device using local storage. We have no access to your conversations. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I report a bad AI response?

You can report any AI response by tapping the report option below the message in the chat. For serious issues, email us directly at support@ullycoffee.com with a description of the response.

Still need help?

If your question isn't covered above, email us at support@ullycoffee.com. Please include:

  • The email address associated with your Ully account
  • The device and OS version you're using (e.g., iPhone 15, iOS 18)
  • A clear description of the issue
  • Screenshots if relevant
