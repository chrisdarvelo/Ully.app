Features

Everything you need.

01 — Ully AI

Coffee expertise, on demand.

Ask anything about espresso, brewing, water chemistry, equipment, or technique. Expert answers powered by Claude.

02 — Dial-in

Point. Analyze. Adjust.

Submit a photo of your extraction. Get real-time AI analysis and precise adjustment recommendations.

03 — Recipes

Your personal recipe library.

Build and organize your coffee recipe collection. Ratios, grind settings, notes — always at hand, stored on your device.

04 — Baristas

Follow the best in the craft.

Curated content from professional baristas. Techniques, posts, and insight from people at the highest level.

05 — Cafes

Save your favorite spots.

Bookmark and organize cafes wherever you go. Build your own personal coffee map.

The right cup for the moment.

Ully reads your local weather and recommends drinks and brew methods accordingly.
