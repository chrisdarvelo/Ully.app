Features

Everything you need.

Ully AI

Ask anything about coffee. Espresso extraction, water chemistry, brew ratios, equipment, technique — expert answers, instantly. Powered by Claude.

Dial-in

Point your camera at your extraction or equipment. Get real-time AI analysis and actionable adjustment recommendations.

Recipe Library

Build and organize your personal coffee recipe collection. Save ratios, grind settings, notes, and brew parameters — always at hand.

Barista Content

Follow professional baristas. Discover techniques, insight, and posts from the people who know coffee at the highest level.

Cafe Map

Save and organize your favorite cafes. Build your personal collection of go-to spots, wherever you are.

Weather-Aware

Ully considers your local weather when recommending drinks and brew methods. The right cup for the right moment.

Coffee is craft. Ully is your expert on demand — from the first dial-in to the perfect pour.
