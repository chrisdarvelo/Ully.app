Delete Your Account

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Delete from the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly from the Ully app. The entire process takes less than a minute.

01

Open the Ully app and make sure you are signed in.

02

Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar.

03

Scroll to the bottom of the screen and tap Delete Account.

04

Enter your password to confirm your identity.

05

Tap Delete to permanently remove your account and all data.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following is permanently removed:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account and login credentials
  • Any files stored in your account (Firebase Storage)
  • All local app data on your device — profile, recipes, saved cafes, barista follows, and chat history

Because your recipes, profile, and all app data are stored only on your device, account deletion removes everything immediately. There is no server-side database to purge separately.

Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to sign in to the app to delete your account, email us and we will process your deletion request within 30 days.

Include the email address associated with your Ully account in your message.

support@ullycoffee.com

After Deletion

  • Your account cannot be recovered after deletion
  • Any data stored locally on your device is also cleared when the account is deleted
  • You may create a new account at any time with the same or a different email address
