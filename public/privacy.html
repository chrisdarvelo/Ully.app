Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026  ·  Effective: February 21, 2026

Introduction

Ully ("we," "our," or "the app") is an AI-powered coffee companion that helps baristas and coffee enthusiasts with espresso extraction, equipment diagnostics, recipes, and brewing guidance. Responses are generated in real-time by an artificial intelligence model (Claude, by Anthropic).

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, what we do not collect, and how we handle your information. We are committed to collecting the absolute minimum data necessary to operate the app.

Age Requirement

Ully is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@ullycoffee.com and we will promptly delete it.

What We Collect

The only information we collect and store on our servers is your email address — used solely to create and authenticate your account via Firebase Authentication. We do not use it for marketing.

Everything else — your profile, recipes, saved cafes, and chat history — is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to our servers.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Chat messages and AI conversations: Your chat history with Ully AI is stored on your device only. We have no access to your conversations.
  • Photos and images: When you use Dial-in or Scan, photos are sent directly to Anthropic's API for real-time analysis. We do not store, upload, or retain your photos on any server. Images are discarded after the AI response is returned.
  • Location or GPS data: We request approximate location permission only to provide weather-aware coffee recommendations. Your location is never stored, logged, or transmitted to our servers.
  • Profile and app data: Your username, role, recipes, saved cafes, and barista follows are stored locally on your device — not on our servers.
  • Date of birth: Used only for the one-time age verification check at signup. It is never stored on our servers or on your device.
  • Behavioral or analytics data: We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • Contacts, call logs, browsing history, or any other device data.

Artificial Intelligence & Generated Content

Ully uses Claude, a large language model developed by Anthropic, to generate responses and analyze photos. This means:

  • All AI responses are generated in real-time and may not always be accurate. Do not rely on Ully for safety-critical decisions.
  • When you send a message or photo, it is transmitted to Anthropic's API for processing. Per Anthropic's API terms, your inputs are not used to train their models and are retained only briefly for trust and safety monitoring. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details.
  • AI responses are proxied through a Firebase Cloud Function — your queries never include your email or account identifiers when sent to Anthropic.

Ully AI is a coffee-specific assistant. It is not a substitute for professional equipment repair, medical, legal, or financial advice.

Camera and Photo Library

Ully requests camera and photo library access only for the Dial-in (extraction analysis) and Scan (part identification) AI features. You may deny these permissions and the rest of the app will continue to function normally.

Photos are never uploaded to our servers, never stored in your account, and never shared with any party other than Anthropic's API for the specific analysis you requested.

How We Use Your Email

Your email address is used solely to:

  • Create and authenticate your Ully account
  • Send a password reset email if you request one

We do not send marketing emails. We do not share your email with third parties for any purpose.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your email and authentication credentials are stored securely with Google Firebase Authentication using industry-standard encryption.
  • All other app data (profile, recipes, cafes, chat history) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • We do not maintain any database of user-generated content, recipes, or conversations.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (Authentication only): Stores your email address and manages secure sign-in. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic (Claude API): Processes your AI chat messages and photos in real-time. Inputs are not used for model training. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • wttr.in (Weather): Provides approximate weather data based on your coarse location. No account or identifier is transmitted.

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or any other third-party data collection tools.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for commercial or marketing purposes. This applies to all users, including California residents under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the email address linked to your account
  • Edit or update your local profile at any time through the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data directly within the app
  • Clear your local chat history at any time through the app
  • Request data deletion by emailing support@ullycoffee.com if you cannot access the app

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal data is collected and the right to opt out of data sales (we do not sell data). EEA/UK residents have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, and portability. Contact support@ullycoffee.com to exercise any of these rights.

Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time from within the app. See our Account Deletion page for step-by-step instructions. If you cannot access the app, email support@ullycoffee.com and we will process your request within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy within the app. Continued use of Ully after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
